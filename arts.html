Tangents

Unlike most siblings, Sven and I met some time at our young age. My father, like many other rulers, had several lovers. Sven is one of his children, just like me, only luckier because of his gender.

His arrival was special to me. He gave me love, warmth, but most importantly freedom. I love him dearly, and so does he. Or so I thought..
